Your independent digital design dance partners!

Kitchen Disco Creative, where original imaginative digital design doesn't need a superstar budget to sparkle.

Say hiheyhello to your KDC Team!

We are two creatives, Ant and Bex, if you will, who cut their teeth in the arts, education, and healthcare sectors.

We're here to bring affordable digital magic to people who have big ideas they are cooking up at home. We are here for you big dreamers, you fearless creators, bedroom selftapers, and future self-made winners.

Think of us as your digital accomplices – we know our way around both pixel-perfect design and purpose-driven projects.

While bigger agencies have the corporate vibe down and charge in the thousands, we keep things refreshingly simple and personal.

We love working with other creative and entrepreneurial people and bouncing ideas around, because truly two (or more) brains are better than one!

I’m Ant, the expert Website Developer.

Not to age me, but I remember the internet launching and have seen all the trends come and go. So I have a lock on how to create the best website for you!

I spent nearly 15 years working in Mental Health, supporting people to live the life they want. However, my interest has always been in the arts. I am an actor and improviser, having trained in and taught improv around the UK and US.

You would think mental health, performing, and websites are wildly different, but what links them is the idea of supporting individuals to be their best selves, and incorporating joy into their lives.

In 2023 I retrained in Web Development, noticing a gap in the market for accessible and affordable web development for the Arts and small business. I am just as passionate about supporting other people’s dreams, as I am about following mine.

Outside of work, I love to travel. I've travelled South America with Bex. Also, watching live music and theatre fills a lot of my spare time.

6'3 if that matters.

I’m Bex, the brains behind the Branding.

You want to know a bit about me? I am half Ecuadorian, half English and living across both hemispheres has certainly shaped who I am.

I would say I am determined, have a great eye, and I’m pretty hilarious (my friends would totally agree).

Having spent a large chunk of time not knowing the who and what of my life, I now embrace all of me and I believe all of us can and should showcase our true unique selves.

I worked in Arts and Education for a decade, picking up a thing two about great branding along the way. When you are trying to keep a classroom full of Gen Z engaged, you better be pulling out the visual big guns!

My design start came, making educational resources for arts and education charities, where I quickly realised that I had an eye for making attention-grabbing content and I really enjoyed the artistic element which was something I had been lacking in my work life.

When not working, I like getting up late, reading tarot, dancing Burlesque, and drinking pints of Guinness.

I like piña coladas and getting caught in the rain.

What makes us stand out on a crowded dance floor?

Team work:

No person is an island, but it can certainly feel like it when striking out on your own in competitive industries. We are here to collaborate with you, it’s your dream but we are here to make it a reality.

Affordability:

Great vibes:

Friends would describe us as ‘nice’ and also ‘dependable’. Just kidding, they actually said “Ant and Bex are creative, fun, have great senses of humour, and make really reasonably priced digital services” Not our words, theirs!

What is the KDC recipe?

Creativity, inclusivity, accessibility.

KDC champions all people! Intersectionality is the future, therefore we want to lift up all voices, but particularly those under-represented in the arts and business: ethnic minorities, LGBTQIA+, persons with disabilities, and persons with neurodiversity.

We are committed to supporting you to elevate your personal brand and helping you stand out in your chosen industry. We are all unique individuals that have a story to tell and so much to offer, so our mission at KDC is to work with you to platform yourself and transform your business into your wildest dreams.

We share your passion, your energy and your good vibes!

Think of us as your creative personal chefs or your digital back-up dancers! We are on your team, and we can’t wait to see you succeed.
