Privacy Policy - Kitchen Disco Creative

Last updated: January 19, 2025

Introduction

Kitchen Disco Creative ('we', 'us', or 'our') is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This document explains our approach to data protection and privacy, specifically addressing how we handle personal data on our website. This policy applies to our website and sub-domains located under kitchendiscocreative.com (the "Website") and the online courses and services we offer (collectively, the "Services"). If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services. If you still have any questions or concerns, please contact us at hiheyhello@kitchendiscocreative.com

Our Data Collection Practices

We are committed to maintaining your privacy and protecting your personal information. Our website has been specifically designed to operate without collecting or storing any personal data from visitors.

What We Don't Collect

For complete transparency, we want to clarify that our website does not:

  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Collect IP addresses
  • Track user behavior or analytics
  • Store any personal information
  • Use contact forms that collect data
  • Maintain user accounts or login systems
  • Process any financial transactions
  • Use third-party analytics tools

Technical Information

Our website is designed to be viewed without the need for any data collection. When you visit our website, the connection is handled by your browser and our web server without storing any personal information about your visit.

Your Rights Under GDPR

Although we don't collect or process personal data, we respect and acknowledge your rights under the GDPR, which include:

  • The right to be informed
  • The right of access
  • The right to rectification
  • The right to erasure
  • The right to restrict processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object
  • Rights in relation to automated decision making and profiling

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other websites. Please note that we have no control over how these third-party websites collect or process your data. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any external websites you visit via links on our website.

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Kitchen Disco Creative

hiheyhello@kitchendiscocreative.com

Supervisory Authority

You have the right to make a complaint at any time to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), the UK supervisory authority for data protection issues (www.ico.org.uk).

Legal Basis for No Data Processing

Under Article 6 of the GDPR, processing of personal data is only lawful if certain conditions are met. As our website does not process any personal data, we are not required to establish a legal basis for data processing.
